#{msg.forums}


In a Forum, which every member of the Manch can start, we can highlight the issues that are important for our nation and need resolution. There are Forum Categories under which a member may start a new Forum. If there is no appropriate Sub Category, the member may select the “OTHER” option. Still, should a member feel that yet another Category (along with Subcategory(ies)) is required altogether, then the member may send an email to the Manch administrator at admin@bjmanch.in ​OR propose the suggestion on Category and its subcategories in the ​Contact Us​ form.
